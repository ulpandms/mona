Privacy Policy

Family Expense Bot is a personal financial recording assistant that operates through WhatsApp.

Data Collection

We collect messages sent by users through WhatsApp for the sole purpose of recording financial transactions into Google Sheets.

Data Usage

Collected data is used only to process and store financial transaction records. We do not sell, share, or distribute user data to third parties.

Data Storage

Financial records are stored securely in Google Sheets associated with the user’s configuration.

User Rights

Users may request deletion of their stored data at any time by contacting us.
